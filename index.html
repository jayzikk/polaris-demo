The Intelligence Gap

Seven systems. No shared vocabulary.

Liberty generates substantial intelligence every day. It cannot assemble that intelligence into decisions. A buying director cannot see, in a single view, which styles are underperforming, which suppliers are creating risk, and how much OTB remains to course-correct. The answer lives across five systems. Reconciliation takes days. The window for action closes in hours.
